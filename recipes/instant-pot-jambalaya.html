Instant Pot Jambalaya

Description

This is a simple, delicious and colorful meal that can be conviniently made in the Instant Pot! Jambalaya is a unique dish with chicken, rice, sausage, vegies and a great combination of spices!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp oil
  • 12 oz of Andouille sausage
  • 1 boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3 green onions
  • 1 green pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 tsp cajun seasoning
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/4 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups of long grain white rice
  • 14.5 oz can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 3/4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Turn the Instant Pot to the saute setting and add in the oil. While that's heating, chop up the chicken and sausage into bite-sized pieced. Then dice the onions and green pepper
  2. Saute the sausage until browned. Remove, then saute the chicken for 1-2 minutes
  3. Turn off the instant pot then add the veggies and seasoning
  4. Stir in the rice, then pour over the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, and salt
  5. Close the instant pot and turn the valve to "sealing." Set to cook for 5 minutes and afterwards wait another 5 minutes before releasing the valve.
  6. Finally, remove the lid, mix everything together, and serve!
